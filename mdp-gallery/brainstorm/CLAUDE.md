# brainstorm — instructions

The raw idea bank. `README.md` here is the **full candidate slate**:
every MDP concept that surfaced during brainstorming, scored on three
axes (manager appeal, visualizability à la Pokémon, pedagogical
richness). `proposals/` holds the subset we chose to flesh out fully.

A candidate earns a full proposal only if it can supply an analogue for
**all seven** ingredients in
[`../reference/pokemon-arc.md`](../reference/pokemon-arc.md) — most
importantly a **tiny drawable state space**, **visible dice**, and a
**state-dependent-optimal twist** (the "evolution" analogue).

## Discipline

If you add a candidate to the slate, score it and say whether it became
a proposal (and why / why not). Keep `README.md` and `proposals/` in
sync — a slate row marked "→ proposal" must have a matching file, and
vice versa.
